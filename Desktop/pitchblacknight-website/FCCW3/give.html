Give

Give in Faith, Grow in Love

"For where your treasure is, there your heart will be also." (Matthew 6:21)

Generosity is a spiritual practice, and we are grateful if you choose to practice it with First Congregational Church of Wilmette. At FCCW we know generosity comes in many forms whether we invest our time, talent or treasure. Donations to FCCW help sustain our ministries and operations as well as support a variety of outside ministries that bring God's love and compassion to ourselves and others.

Give using Zelle

Using this method means that more of your gift goes toward our mission as there are no fees with Zelle (Credit Card payments deduct a fee).

Just one small step before you send us a contribution via Zelle. We want to know who you are, how to thank you and where we can send your tax letter (your donation is tax deductible). Please fill this out first!

This is not a payment! The next screen will let you know the Zelle address to send the payment to.

Of course, if you don't use Zelle, please feel free to give using a Credit Card, PayPal or check.

Give by Check

To donate by check, please mail your donation to:

First Congregational Church of Wilmette
Attn: Donations
1125 Wilmette Ave, Wilmette, IL 60091

Note: If you are giving to a specific effort, please make a note in the memo line of your check.

Give with Credit Card or PayPal

Securely give by credit card or PayPal. Your generosity makes all we do possible.

We accept Credit Card and PayPal.

Stock Transfer

If you wish to make a stock transfer, please contact amoly@fccw.org for specific instructions.
