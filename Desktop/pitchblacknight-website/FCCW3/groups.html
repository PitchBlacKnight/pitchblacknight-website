Music Ministry

Music is central to worship at FCCW. Our choir rehearses Wednesday evenings and sings most Sundays. Directed by Katherine Hughes. Instrumentalists and vocalists of all levels are welcome — no audition required.

Email the Music Director →

Craft & Chat

Bring your favorite craft to this warm, welcoming group, every Thursday in the Library from 1:00–3:00 PM. Knitters, quilters, and crafters of all kinds gather for good company and creative time. No experience necessary.

Sign Up for Craft & Chat Alerts →

Spiritual Wellness

Nurturing body, mind, and spirit — FCCW offers sound bath meditation, wisdom walks in nature, and other contemplative practices throughout the year. All are welcome, regardless of background or tradition.

Sign Up for Wellness Updates →

Helping the Unhoused

FCCW partners with Connections for the Homeless in Evanston. Our members volunteer at the shelter and help prepare and serve meals — showing up in a tangible way for people in their most vulnerable moments. No special skills required, just a willingness to show up.

Sign Up to Volunteer →

Book Discussion Group

Our Book Discussion Group meets monthly to explore fiction, nonfiction, memoir, and more. It's a space for open conversation, diverse perspectives, and genuine community. New members always welcome.

Sign Up for Book Group Updates →

Meal Preparation

Volunteers help prepare and serve meals for community programs throughout the year — from Thanksgiving baskets to summer gatherings. A tangible way to feed neighbors and build friendship at the same time.

Sign Up for Meal Prep Alerts →

Gardening Team

Our Gardening Team tends the church grounds, cultivating beauty and calm for everyone who passes through. Whether you have a green thumb or just love being outside, all are welcome to help plant, weed, and grow.

Sign Up to Garden →

Sanctuary Decorating Team

The Sanctuary Decorating Team transforms our worship space for each season of the church year — from the purples of Lent to the greens of ordinary time. Creative souls of all kinds are welcome to help make our space reflect the spirit of the season.
