FCCW Craft and Chat combines socializing and creativity.

Bring your favorite craft to this group, every Thursday in the Library from 1-3. Or just come and chat, and give positive feedback to the artists and crafters.

I need some new creative friends. Send me info. Here is your chance to combine creativity and socializing.

Meditation, Prayer and Spiritual Wellness Opportunities at FCCW

You'll find a mix of opportunities for exploring spiritual wellness at FCCW, from contemplative prayer to sound bath, to yoga, to nature walks.

When is the next FCCW event? I need inner peace. We will send you notice of upcoming Spiritual Wellness events.

Helping the Unhoused

Our church has supported several homeless agencies over the past decades. Currently we offer various types of volunteer opportunities with Connections for the Homeless in Evanston.

How can I help? Send me more information. Join with our group for a one-time event at the homeless shelter.

FCCW Book Discussion Group Brings Together Book Lovers for Interesting Discussions.

The Book Discussion Group meets on the third Thursday of each month at 3:00 p.m. in the Library. The discussion is a convergence of opinions and ideas in a spirit of camaraderie and fun. All are welcome.

Sign up to get our monthly Book Alert. Find out meeting date, time and the next book we will be discussing.

Tap Your Creativity with Our Sanctuary Decorating Team.

Use flowers, plants, fabrics, stones, lights or other materials to decorate our worship space. Set your imagination free.

Sign up for our Decorating Calendar. We'll notify you about upcoming opportunities and team meetings for sanctuary decorating.

FCCW Needs Your Talent for Photography and Video

If you enjoy photography or video we want you on our team. We need photos for our website, our email newsletter, and to document our activities over the year. And we'll put your video talents to work for streaming our worship services, plus adult education events.

How can I participate? Send me details. See a great photo? Grab it! And we can always use help with our streaming.

Music Opportunities

Join the Choir

The FCCW Choir rehearses on Wednesday nights, and performs Sunday morning at worship.

Sing or Play Your Instrument

We have lots of opportunities for you to share your musical talents for one-time special performances. String, bass and woodwind players, guitarists, keyboard players and percussionists can find a place in our worship plans.

I want to know more about FCCW musical opportunities. We will send you information about the choir, and one-time events.

Enjoy Gardening? FCCW Gardening Team needs you!

We need willing gardeners to keep our outdoor spaces beautiful. It can be a one-time commitment, or a regular outing. Join our team!

When is the next gardening day? Send me info. Gardening opportunities are flexible – do a little bit, or be a team regular.
