Our Vision & Mission

"Helping people connect to faith, to each other, and to the world."

At FCCW, we believe faith is a journey, not a destination. We are a community of seekers, thinkers, and doers — progressive, welcoming, and rooted in 150 years of grace.

Open & Affirming

Everyone is welcome
at our table.

FCCW celebrates open communion on the first Sunday of each month. All are welcome — regardless of background, faith history, sexuality, or identity. No exceptions. No prerequisites.

We are an Open & Affirming congregation of the United Church of Christ. That's not a policy — it's who we are.

Find Your Place

There's a way for everyone to belong.

Music Ministry
Our choir and instrumental ensemble bring worship to life. Singers and musicians of all levels welcome — no audition required. Directed by Katherine Hughes.
Helping the Unhoused
FCCW partners with Connections for the Homeless in Evanston. Volunteers help prepare and serve meals — no special skills needed, just a willingness to show up.
Book Discussion Group
Monthly discussions exploring fiction, nonfiction, memoir, and more. A space for open conversation, diverse perspectives, and genuine community.
