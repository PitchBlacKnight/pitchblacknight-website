Sharing Our Space

To all who strive to make a difference ~

There's a place, a room for you here.

First Congregational Church of Wilmette is a community dedicated to doing good.

In the heart of downtown Wilmette, we strive to be a welcoming home for those committed to making a difference. Open to all — we provide a shared space where you can connect, collaborate, and create a better future together.
