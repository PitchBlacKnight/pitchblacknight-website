About Bert
A career built
on genuine
connection.

Before Bert Gillette became one of Denver's most trusted real estate advisors, he spent years doing something harder — and more meaningful.

He facilitated outdoor adventures for people with disabilities, designed therapeutic programs for veteran families, and coordinated a nationwide alliance of eighty non-profits. These weren't just careers — they were a masterclass in listening, adaptability, and putting people first.

Today, Bert brings that same human-centered energy to Denver's real estate market. He stays ahead of the curve through continuing education, deep neighborhood knowledge, and genuine engagement with the communities he calls home.
