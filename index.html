04 — DAILY WISDOM

"The most radical act is to be entirely, unapologetically yourself in a world that profits from your self-doubt."

I learned to edit

the thaw—

not the freeze.

Every medium taught me

a different way to be wrong

until it was right.

The boundaries dissolve

when you stop

asking permission.

Still learning.

Still making.

That might be the whole point.
