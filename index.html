The Person

The Human.

I am a Fluxus artist.
Not a brand. Not a title.
A living practice.

Every medium is my medium. Digital, physical, interpretive, sonic — the boundaries between them are the first thing I dissolve.

My name is Mikel Rosenthal. I go by PBK — short for PitchBlacKnight. I live in Chicago Heights with my husband Brandon and our black cat Hexadecimal — HEX — a rescue who showed up three Halloweens ago and never left.

I am a polymath. My music is first-take improv — one pass, live mastered, the imperfection is the craft. My client work is the opposite: I envision the finished result almost immediately, we clarify it together, I map the path, and I execute until it's exactly right. Every client I have ever worked with has been completely satisfied. I am proud of that record.

I have hyper-fantasia: an inner world so vivid and texturally rich it operates as a parallel reality. I am a phonetic mimic who can absorb and reproduce accents, dialects, animal sounds, and mechanical textures after a single exposure. I pick up languages with unnerving speed — not just words but the music of how they're spoken.

I spent 30 years managing chronic pain. Theater of the Mind changed that. What I build now comes from the other side of that door.

I
Anti-Separation

Art is not separate from life. Work is not separate from art. Every output — a website, a song, a space, a word — is the same gesture, refracted differently.

II
Total Fluency

Polymath is not a resume word. It's a condition. Forty-five years spent refusing to specialize means every discipline informs every other. The edges blur. That's the point.

III
Conscious Output

Every piece of work enters the world with intention. If it doesn't serve something larger — environmentally, socially, spiritually — it doesn't leave the studio.
