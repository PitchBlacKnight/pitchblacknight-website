The Person

The Human.

My name is Mikel Rosenthal. I go by PBK — short for PitchBlacKnight. I live in Chicago Heights with my husband Brandon and our black cat Hexadecimal — HEX — a rescue who showed up three Halloweens ago and never left.

I am a Fluxus artist. A polymath. I produce music, design systems, create motion art, and build the spaces where all of it lives together.

Everything I make starts the same way: first take, no blueprint, no rehearsal. Then I master it live. The imperfection is the craft — the breath of the moment, not the sterility of infinite takes.

I spent 30 years managing chronic pain. Theater of the Mind changed that. What I build now comes from the other side of that door.
