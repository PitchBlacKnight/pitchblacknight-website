The Concept

A new kind of
private retreat

OurDens is one of the most sought-after adults-only retreats in the Pacific Northwest — forty private cabins suspended in old-growth forest above the Columbia River, each one engineered on pillar foundations that leave the forest floor completely undisturbed. The habitat beneath each cabin is intact. The silence is total. Children are not permitted on property.


A winding private road leads to the main lodge. Your personal attendant meets you at arrival, escorts you by golf cart to your den, and has already placed your bags exactly where you asked. One number to call — or a QR code scan — and anything you need arrives. Guests return year after year. Most are already booked for next season before they check out.

Leave No Trace

Built with the
land, not on it

OurDens was designed from the ground up — literally — to leave the Pacific Northwest ecosystem intact. Every design decision begins with a single question: how do we ensure the forest doesn't know we're here?

01
Steel Framework, Elevated Off Ground

Every cabin rests on precision-drilled steel pillar foundations — roots, habitat, and drainage undisturbed beneath. The forest floor doesn't know we're here.

02
Solar Roofing & Insulated Panels

Integrated solar cells, vacuum insulation panels, and structural insulated panels (SIPs) minimize energy consumption. Low-E windows maximize thermal performance without sacrificing views.

03
Reclaimed & Recycled Materials

Reclaimed wood, recycled wood-plastic composite lumber, insulating concrete forms, and locally sourced supplies — chosen for longevity, not just aesthetics.
